Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Tempus application (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by the Tempus open-source contributors (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free, open-source, and libre service. This service is provided "AS IS".


Zero Data Collection & Telemetry

The Application respects your privacy completely. The Application does not collect, store, track, transmit, or process any personal data, telemetry, analytics, usage statistics, diagnostic reports, or unique device identifiers. Nothing you do within the Application is logged or sent to the Service Provider or any third party.


Network Connections & Self-Hosted Servers

The Application operates solely as a client interface. All network traffic generated by the Application consists of direct communication between your mobile device and the self-hosted server(s) (such as a Subsonic-compatible server or Internet radio stream) that you explicitly configure in the settings.

  • No data passes through any intermediate servers managed by the Service Provider.
  • Your credentials, server URLs, and account information remain stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to anyone other than your designated server.

Third-Party Access & Services

The Application does not contain any third-party SDKs, tracking libraries, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks. No data is ever shared with, sold to, or accessible by third parties.


Permissions

The Application only requests device permissions strictly necessary to perform core audio playback functions (such as network access to stream music from your server, and foreground service permissions for media control notification banners). These permissions are never used to harvest data.


Children’s Privacy

Because the Application collects absolutely no personal information or location data from anyone, it complies fully with privacy protection regulations regarding children and does not solicit data from users of any age.


Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. However, the core principle of zero data collection will remain unchanged. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any updates.


Source Code & Transparency

Tempus is free and open-source software (FLOSS/Libre). You are welcome to inspect, review, and audit the source code directly on GitHub to verify our privacy compliance and data practices.


Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please open an issue or contact the maintainers via the official project repository.
